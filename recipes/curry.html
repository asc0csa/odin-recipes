Curry

Back to Recipes Page

Description

Its pretty good.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Curry Block
  • Beef Chunks
  • White rice

Steps

  1. Cut potato, carrots, and onions into bite size pieces.
  2. Boil a pot of water.
  3. Add the beef chunks and the veggies.
  4. Mix the curry block into the boiling water.
  5. Simmer and mix for 20 minutes.
  6. Pour curry on top of rice.
