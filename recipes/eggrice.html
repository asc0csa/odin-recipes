Eggs and Rice

Back to Recipes Page

Description

Protein and carbs to start the day.

Ingredients

  • Cooked white rice
  • A egg
  • Cooking oil
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Put some oil in a pan on medium heat.
  2. Crack the egg into the pan.
  3. Cook the egg until the whites are visible.
  4. Put egg on top of the rice.
  5. Pour soy sauce on top.
